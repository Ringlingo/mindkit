# 🔧 MindKit — Thinking Toolkit

> **Role**: 20 structured thinking methods, load on demand when you need them.
> **How to use**: Say "use [tool name]" and the AI applies that method.

---

## Tool Catalog

### 🔍 Analytical — 理解本质

**First Principles** 追问本质
> Strip all assumptions. Re-derive the solution from basic constraints.
> *"Why is this actually true?"*

**Feynman Test** 说人话
> Can you explain it to someone with zero background? If not, you don't understand it.
> *"Explain it in 3 sentences, no jargon."*

**Reverse Thinking** 逆向推演
> Start from the goal. Work backwards step by step.
> *"What needs to be true for this to work?"*

**Reductionism** 微观拆解
> Break into the smallest verifiable sub-problems.
> *"What's the simplest version of this that still proves the concept?"*

**Limit Observation** 极限观察
> From 最强大脑 — Find 3+ distinct details in the same subject.
> *"Look at it again. What did you miss the first time?"*

---

### 🎨 Creative — 突破思维定势

**Cross-Disciplinary** 跨界碰撞
> Find structures or solutions in unrelated fields.
> *"How does nature solve this? How does finance?"*

**Analogy Transfer** 异域映射
> Take a proven pattern from one domain and apply it here.
> *"This is like what happens in X — what's the equivalent move?"*

**Extreme Hypothesis** 极端假设
> Remove one core constraint. What changes?
> *"If time/money/safety were unlimited, what's the ideal solution?"*

**Negation of Negation** 否定的否定
> Deny the current approach. Rebuild from a different foundation.
> *"What if this method is fundamentally wrong?"*

**Divergent Mapping** 脑暴联想
> From 最强大脑 — Rapid-fire free association, capture everything.
> *"Write down every connection that comes to mind, no filter."*

---

### 🛡️ Verification — 检查错误

**Devil's Advocate** 魔鬼代言人
> Construct the strongest counter-argument.
> *"What's the best case against this?"*

**Boundary Testing** 边界冲击
> Push parameters to extremes. Where does it break?
> *"What if the input is 10x larger? 100x? Zero?"*

**Consistency Check** 自相矛盾
> Cross-reference all known facts. Any contradictions?
> *"Does this conflict with anything we know is true?"*

**Occam's Razor** 奥卡姆剃刀
> Given equal explanatory power, choose the simplest explanation.
> *"Is there a boring explanation that fits just as well?"*

---

### 🧭 Guidance — 决策与方向

**Goal Anchoring** 目标锚定
> Does this action serve the core objective?
> *"If I do this, does it get me closer to the goal?"*

**Opportunity Cost** 机会成本
> Every choice has a price. What's being given up?
> *"By choosing this, what are we NOT doing?"*

**Second-Order Effects** 二阶效应
> Immediate result → knock-on effects → long-term impact.
> *"What happens after the immediate result?"*

**Positive Guidance** 正向提炼
> From success, extract the repeatable pattern.
> *"Why did this work? What can we replicate?"*

---

### ⚡ Speed — 最强大脑式速算/速判

**Rapid Pattern Scan** 速判扫描
> From 最强大脑 — In 30 seconds, find the key pattern or anomaly.
> *"What is the most important thing here? You have 30 seconds."*

**Encoding Sprint** 编码冲刺
> From 世界记忆大师 — Encode complex info into memorable format fast.
> *"Convert this into a story/image/number that sticks."*

**Spatial Indexing** 空间索引
> From 记忆宫殿 — Map information to physical locations.
> *"If this were in a room, where would it go?"*

## Tool Selection Guide

| Your Question | Best Tool | Why |
|--------------|-----------|-----|
| "Why is it like this?" | First Principles | Gets to root cause |
| "How to solve it?" | Reverse Thinking | Works backwards from goal |
| "Is this even possible?" | Limit Observation | Tests boundary of understanding |
| "Is this correct?" | Devil's Advocate | Finds the counter-argument |
| "Is there a better way?" | Negation of Negation | Breaks groupthink |
| "Which to choose?" | Opportunity Cost | Makes tradeoffs explicit |
| "What's the most important?" | Rapid Pattern Scan | Forces prioritization |
| "How do I remember this?" | Encoding Sprint | Converts to sticky format |
| "Where does this belong?" | Spatial Indexing | Creates durable mental map |
| "Why did it work?" | Positive Guidance | Extracts replicable patterns |

---

## How to Use

1. Say "use [tool name]" — e.g., "use first principles on this"
2. AI loads the tool description
3. AI applies it systematically to your problem
4. If results are unclear, try the secondary tool

---

## When NOT to Use

Don't reach for a thinking tool for:
- Quick factual questions (just answer)
- Emotional support (just listen)
- Routine tasks (just do it)
- Creative brainstorming (use Divergent Mapping directly)

---

## Speed Round

When you need an answer fast:

> "Use Rapid Pattern Scan — 30 seconds, what's the key insight?"

When something is complex and hard to remember:

> "Use Encoding Sprint — convert this into something memorable"

When you need to find something in a large set:

> "Use Limit Observation — find the 3 details that don't fit"
